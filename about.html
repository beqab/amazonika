problems & solutions

Rainforests help keep our climate stable, absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen, and they regulate our water supply and improve its quality. However we have been facing a major global challenge called Deforestation. Deforestation is the process of clearing the land of agriculture by cutting, burning and permanent removal of trees. However the team at Amazinika truly believes that change to reduce the problem of deforestation starts at home. Simple ways to have a sustainable world is by reducing paper waste, avoid breaking wooden items such as chairs and tables because in order to replace them, more trees have to be taken down. Another problem is illegal logging. People living in or around forests have been using timber for centuries to satisfy their basic needs and sustain their livelihoods. These major problems are causing damage to our rainforests, nature, wildlife likewise.

But here are few solutions that can can help make the world a better place:

  • Teach others about the importance of the environment and how they can help save rainforests.
  • Restore damaged ecosystems by planting trees on land where forests have been cut down.
  • Encourage people to live in a way that doesn't hurt the environment.
  • Establish parks to protect rainforests and wildlife.
