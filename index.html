What is Amazonika and what makes it special?

Amazonika is a sustainable community driven project to protect our rainforests. Moreover it is a yield-generating social cryptocurrency. It’s value lies not only in its currency, but in its ability to generate positive long-term externalities for the planet through charitable projects. By becoming a part of Amazonika you’re not only supporting our mission of embittering the world but also profit alongside the whole journey.

What’s the 30% burn?

We burned 30% of the initial supply. Like that we created a deflationary system.

How to manually add my token to my trust/ metamask wallet?

Trust Wallet : Click on settings. Scroll to the bottom and click “Add custom token”. Make sure the network is set on “Smart Chain” and paste the below information:

  • Name: AMAZONIKA
  • Contract: „XXX“
  • Symbol: AMAZONIKA
  • Decimals: 9

Metamask: Scroll to the bottom and click “Add Token”. Click on “Custom-Token” and paste in the contract address: “XXX”
Click on Continue and Add Token.

Do I have to switch my Metamask/ Trust Wallet to Binance Smart Chain or can I leave it on Ethereum Mainnet?

You have to switch it to Binance Smart Chain.

How are the transaction taxes distributed?

On every transaction a tax of 10% is applied. It will be distributed as following: 4% will be reallocated to charity, 3% to liquidity pool, 2% among all token holders and 1% to marketing.

Do I buy Amazonika over v1 or v2 on pancakeswap?
